Privacy Policy for Multiple Clipboard

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Multiple Clipboard Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Multiple Clipboard is designed to enhance your clipboard experience by storing multiple copied texts. To provide this functionality, our extension collects and stores the following information:

  • Text content that you copy while browsing websites
  • Timestamps of when the text was copied

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for the purpose of providing the core functionality of the Multiple Clipboard extension:

  • To store your clipboard history
  • To display your clipboard history when you open the extension popup
  • To allow you to search through your clipboard history
  • To enable filtering of clipboard items by date

Local Storage

All data collected by Multiple Clipboard is stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage API. The data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

Data Retention and User Control

Your clipboard history is retained in local storage until you choose to delete it. You have complete control over your data and can:

  • Delete individual clipboard items at any time
  • Clear your entire clipboard history with the "Clear All" button
  • Filter and manage your clipboard items as needed

If you uninstall the extension, all stored clipboard data will be permanently deleted.

Third-Party Access

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your clipboard data to any third parties. Your data remains exclusively on your device and under your control.
